Copyright Claims

SaveAnyMedia respects copyright law and will respond promptly to valid claims of copyright infringement.

How to Submit a Claim

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used without permission, please provide the following information via email to support@saveanymedia.com:

  • Your contact information (name, address, phone, email)
  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  • The URL(s) of the material you claim is infringing
  • A statement that you believe in good faith the use is not authorized
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Response to Claims

Upon receiving a valid claim, SaveAnyMedia will take appropriate actions including removing the infringing content.
